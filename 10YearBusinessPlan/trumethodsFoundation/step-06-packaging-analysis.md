## DOCUMENT OVERVIEW

**Title:** TruMethods Framework Foundations - Step 6: Packaging Your Service Offering

**Core Methodology:** The "Chocolate Cake" approach to service packaging - transforming technical "ingredients" into business outcomes that clients understand and value. Move from selling components to selling complete solutions with defined edges, clear expectations, and predictable delivery.

**Key Insight:** MSPs fail when they sell "ingredients" (antivirus, backup, monitoring). They succeed when they sell "cake" - complete business outcomes delivered through a standardized recipe combining ingredients with "your company way."

**Impact on Venturer Technology:** This methodology directly addresses Venturer's critical pricing transformation challenge ($45 to $150/seat). Current service delivery is already sophisticated (Kristyn's documentation, proactive monitoring) but needs repackaging from technical components into business outcomes that justify premium pricing.
