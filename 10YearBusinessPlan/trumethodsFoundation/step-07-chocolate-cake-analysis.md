# TruMethods Step 07: Chocolate Cake Implementation Roadmap

## DOCUMENT OVERVIEW

**Title:** TruMethods Framework Foundations - Step 7: Chocolate Cake

**Core Methodology:** Selling and delivering managed services as a complete business outcome (chocolate cake) rather than individual technical components (ingredients)

**Key Insight:** MSPs must stop selling technical ingredients (antivirus, backup, monitoring) and start selling business outcomes (productivity, reduced risk, peace of mind) through a unified vision and proven delivery process.

**Impact on Venturer Technology:** This methodology directly addresses Venturer's critical need to transition from $45/seat commodity pricing to $150/seat value-based pricing by fundamentally changing how services are positioned, sold, and delivered.
