# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

*Last Updated: 2026-01-01*

## What This Is

KPIs are the vital signs of your life and progress toward goals. Focus on metrics that actually matter, not vanity metrics.

**Key Principle:** What gets measured gets managed. But only measure what truly reflects your mission and values.

## Review Schedule

### Weekly (15 min)
- Review all life balance KPIs
- Check goal-specific metrics
- Update trends

### Monthly (30 min)
- Calculate all monthly KPIs
- Look for patterns
- Adjust targets if needed

### Quarterly (1 hour)
- Full KPI assessment
- Retire metrics that don't matter
- Add new metrics if gaps exist
