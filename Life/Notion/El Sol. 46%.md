# What did I think.

### Summary

El Sol is a whisky that delivers a complex and warming flavor profile, showcasing rich, earthy characteristics with a hint of sweetness. It pairs beautifully with cured meats like prosciutto and would complement a range of savory dishes. This is a refined spirit, perfect for slow sipping and contemplative moments.

### Tasting Notes

1. **Appearance**: Deep amber with golden hues, inviting and rich in color.
2. **Nose**: Aromas of leather and dried currants dominate upon opening, with subtle undertones of toasted oak and spices.
3. **Palate**: A warm, full-bodied whisky that immediately coats the mid-tongue with a velvety texture. Flavors of dark fruits, caramel, and a whisper of smokiness emerge.
4. **Finish**: Long and satisfying, with hints of dark chocolate and a gentle spice lingering on the palate.
5. **Balance**: Well-rounded, with a harmonious blend of sweetness and warmth that enhances its depth.
6. **Mouthfeel**: Smooth and slightly oily, leaving a lasting impression.
7. **Complexity**: Layers of flavors unfold with each sip, offering a sophisticated tasting experience.
8. **Pairing**: Best enjoyed with prosciutto, aged cheeses, or roasted nuts for a complementary flavor profile.
9. **Occasion**: Ideal for special occasions or as a contemplative drink to savor slowly.
10. **Overall Impression**: A whisky that leaves a lasting impression with its rich character and versatility.

### Food Recommendations

- Thinly sliced prosciutto or other cured meats
- Aged cheddar or Gouda cheese
- Dark chocolate with a high cocoa content
- Roasted almonds or pecans
- Grilled lamb chops with a rosemary glaze
