### Test The Impossible

1. **What if I did the opposite for 48 Hours? What would that look like?**

  

  

2. **What do I spend a silly amount of money on? How might I improve that / scratch my own itch?**

  

  

3. **What would I do/have/be if I had $10 million? What’s my real Target Monthly Income?** (See [fourhourworkweek.com/tmi](http://fourhourworkweek.com/tmi))

  

  

4. **What are the worst things that could happen? Could I get back here?** _(See “fear-setting”)_

  

  

5. **If I could only work 2 hours per week on my business, what would I do?**

  

  

**6. What are my big problems / challenges at the moment?**

  

**What if I could only subtract to solve them? Instead of 'What should I do?, think 'What should I simplify?'...**

  

  

7. **What am I feeling Stretched or overwhelmed by at the moment?**

**What would this look like if it were easy?**
