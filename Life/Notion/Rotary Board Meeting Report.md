## International Projects Director's Report

Dear Board Members,

I am pleased to present our International Projects report for this quarter. Our club continues to make significant impacts globally through our partnership with Someone Who Cares and individual member-led initiatives.

### Project Overview:

- **Cameroon:** Shipped a 40-foot high-top container of supplies.
- **Papua New Guinea (PNG):**
    - Sent a 20-foot container to Pikinini Haus
    - Delivered two 20-foot containers to Mount Hargan Hospital
    - Shipped a 20-foot container of school supplies to a Seventh Day Adventist School in Lae
- **Solomon Islands:**
    - Sent two 20-foot containers to Helena Goldie Hospital
    - Included a solar water treatment plant, unloaded in Honiara
    - Provided 20 boxes of materials for a women's shelter in Noro

Our club members have been actively involved in sorting and packing items for these shipments, demonstrating their commitment to our international service efforts.

Additionally, Ricky Prout continues to work with PNG IMR and the Malaria Research team on their IT systems upgrade. We are also researching for Microscope repair options to help the lab with their equipment needs.

I want to express my gratitude to all club members who have contributed their time and effort to these projects. Your dedication is making a real difference in communities around the world.

Respectfully submitted,

Kristyn Prout

International Director
