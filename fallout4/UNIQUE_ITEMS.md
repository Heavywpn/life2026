# Unique Weapons & Armor

All unique items with fixed legendary effects that can't be found elsewhere.

## Tier List Key
- **S-Tier:** Best in class, game-changing
- **A-Tier:** Excellent, worth getting
- **B-Tier:** Good, situationally useful
- **C-Tier:** Okay, mostly for collection

## Priority Acquisition Order

### Early Game (Level 1-15)
1. **Righteous Authority** - Free from "Call to Arms" quest
2. **Deliverer** - Join Railroad, complete "Tradecraft"
3. **Overseer's Guardian** - Buy from Vault 81 (save up 2000 caps)
4. **Kellogg's Pistol** - Main quest, Fort Hagen
5. **Pickman's Blade** - Pickman's Gift quest

### Mid Game (Level 15-30)
6. **Spray n' Pray** - Find Cricket traveling merchant
7. **Kremvh's Tooth** - Dunwich Borers (prepare for spooky)
8. **Grognak's Axe** - Hubris Comics
9. **Furious Power Fist** - Kill Swan at Boston Common
10. **Le Fusil Terribles** - Libertalia

### Late Game (Level 30+)
11. **The Last Minute** - Castle after "Old Guns"
12. **Big Boy** - Save up ~14000 caps
13. **Freefall Legs** - Requires jetpack power armor
14. **Silver Shroud Armor** (upgraded) - Complete full quest chain
15. **X-01 Power Armor** - Check National Guard, Court 35

## Weapons by Damage Type

### Best Explosive Weapons
1. Spray n' Pray (common ammo)
2. Big Boy (nukes)
3. Partystarter (missiles)

### Best Energy Weapons
1. Righteous Authority (crits)
2. Old Faithful (opener damage)
3. Final Judgment (sustained fire)

### Best Stealth Weapons
1. Deliverer (VATS/AP efficiency)
2. Pickman's Blade (sneak melee)
3. Tinker Tom Special (sneak rifle)

### Best VATS Weapons
1. Deliverer (reduced AP cost)
2. Kellogg's Pistol (AP refill on crit)
3. Overseer's Guardian (double projectile = double damage)
