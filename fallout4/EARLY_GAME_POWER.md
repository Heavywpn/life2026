# Early Game Power Guide

Follow this checklist to become overpowered in the first few hours.

## Phase 1: Vault 111 & Sanctuary (Level 1-2)

- [x] **Vault 111**: Grab all items, check every room
- [ ] **Vault 111**: Note Cryolator in Overseer's office (return with Dogmeat later)
- [x] **Sanctuary - Your House**: Pick up "You're SPECIAL!" book under Shaun's crib (+1 SPECIAL)
- [x] **Sanctuary**: Scrap everything - fences, tires, trees, broken houses
- [x] **Sanctuary**: Collect all Pre-War Money (valuable for selling)
- [x] **Sanctuary**: Check every container, especially the Root Cellar behind a house
- [x] **Codsworth**: Talk to him, he knows where to find supplies

## Phase 2: Red Rocket & Concord (Level 2-5)

- [x] **Red Rocket**: Recruit Dogmeat
- [x] **Red Rocket**: Grab Fusion Core in basement (locked room)
- [x] **Red Rocket**: Collect all items, good early crafting station
- [x] **Concord - Museum of Freedom**: Help Preston's group
- [x] **Concord**: Get the Laser Musket (free, decent early weapon)
- [x] **Concord**: Grab Power Armor from rooftop (first set!)
- [x] **Concord**: Get Minigun from Vertibird
- [x] **Concord**: Kill the Deathclaw (easy in Power Armor with Minigun)
- [x] **Concord**: Loot all Raiders for gear/caps

## Phase 3: Dogmeat Exploits (Level 3+)

- [ ] **Cryolator Glitch**: Take Dogmeat to Vault 111, command him to "fetch" items near the Cryolator case - he can grab it through the glass!
- [ ] **Duplication**: Dogmeat can duplicate items (search current patch status)

## Phase 4: Power Locations (Level 5-10)

### Easy Unique Weapons
- [ ] **Righteous Authority** (Laser Rifle): Complete "Call to Arms" with Paladin Danse at Cambridge Police Station
- [ ] **Overseer's Guardian** (Combat Rifle): Buy from Alexis at Vault 81 (~2000 caps) - BEST early gun
- [ ] **Deliverer** (10mm Pistol): Join Railroad, complete "Tradecraft" quest
- [ ] **Spray n' Pray** (Submachine Gun): Buy from Cricket the traveling merchant (~2000 caps)
- [ ] **Big Boy** (Fat Man): Buy from Arturo in Diamond City (~14000 caps)

### Early Bobbleheads
- [x] **Perception Bobblehead**: Museum of Freedom, same building as Preston's group
- [ ] **Repair Bobblehead**: Corvega Assembly Plant (hostile but doable at level 5-8)

### Early Magazines
- [ ] **Wasteland Survival Guide #9** (Sanctuary): +5% XP from cooking, in Root Cellar
- [ ] **Grognak the Barbarian** (Sanctuary): In one of the houses
- [ ] **RobCo Fun** (various): Holotape games, sell for caps

## Phase 5: Diamond City & Power Farming (Level 10-15)

- [ ] **Diamond City**: Access main trading hub, tons of merchants
- [ ] **Home Plate**: Buy a house in Diamond City (2000 caps) for city base
- [ ] **Nick Valentine**: Rescue him for a great companion and detective skills
- [ ] **Piper**: Interview with her for Piper as companion
- [ ] **Commonwealth Bank**: South of Diamond City, lots of Pre-War Money

### Adhesive Farm Setup (Critical for Crafting)
At any settlement with water:
- [ ] Plant Corn (2 per batch)
- [ ] Plant Mutfruit (3 per batch)
- [ ] Plant Tato (2 per batch)
- [ ] Build Water Purifier
- [ ] Craft Vegetable Starch at cooking station = 5 Adhesive!

## XP Farming Spots

1. **Idiot Savant + Quicksave**: Save before turning in quests, reload until it procs
2. **Settlement Building**: Each item placed gives XP
3. **Cooking**: Mass-produce items with Idiot Savant active
4. **Super Mutant Camps**: High XP enemies, respawn after time

## Early Money Making

1. **Water Purifiers**: Build many at Sanctuary, collect Purified Water, sell it
2. **Jet Production**: Fertilizer + Plastic = Jet (sells for 40+ caps each)
3. **Pre-War Money**: Collect and sell, each worth 8 caps
4. **Mutfruit**: Grows fast, sells for 8 caps each

## Combat Tips for Early Game

1. Use VATS constantly - slows time and shows enemy locations
2. Target legs on fast enemies (Ghouls, Dogs)
3. Target Combat Inhibitors on robots
4. Grenades/Molotovs are overpowered early
5. Sneak attacks do 2x damage (more with perks)
6. Power Armor makes you nearly invincible but uses Fusion Cores
