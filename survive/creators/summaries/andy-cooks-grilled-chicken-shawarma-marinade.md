### Recipe: Shawarma-Style Grilled Chicken Thighs

**Marinade Ingredients:**
- Yogurt (base)
- Lemon zest
- Lemon juice
- Cumin

- Sumac
- Garlic

**Alternative Simple Marinade:**

- Dried oregano
- Lemon zest
- Garlic
- Chili flakes

**Marination Time**: 2 hours in fridge (can go overnight, but watch citrus levels)

**Grilled Vegetables:**
- Asparagus (whole)
- Red onion (large petal shapes)
- Zucchini (quartered, seeds removed)

**Vegetable Prep:**
- Olive oil and salt only
- Finish with lemon juice/zest and olive oil after grilling

### Key Cooking Techniques

**Chicken:**
- Use boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- Place smoother side (non-bone side) down first for better color
- Don't move too much initially - let it settle
- Cook ~10 minutes total
- Target temp: 62°C (Andy's preference) vs 70°C (health authority recommendation)
- Lemon juice in marinade tenderizes meat (makes flipping trickier)

**Vegetables:**
- Keep asparagus and zucchini whole for better control
- Remove zucchini seeds from large ones (prevents mushiness - texture matters)
- Cook onions on flat top (won't fall through grates)
- Vegetables are done when they start to "sag" (become flimsy)
- Carryover cooking applies to vegetables too

**Barbecue/Grill:**
- Gas barbecue (most common in Australia)
- Use both grill side and flat top
- Cook with lid closed (retains heat better)
- Australian barbecues designed for closed-lid cooking

### Pan Selection Guidance

**Protein-to-Pan Recommendations:**
- **Steak**: Carbon steel pan (Andy's preference) or cast iron
- **Fish**: Stainless steel pan
- **Eggs**: Stainless steel pan
- **Chicken**: Carbon steel pan
- **High-acid sauces** (tomato, etc.): Avoid cast iron/carbon steel (deteriorates seasoning)
