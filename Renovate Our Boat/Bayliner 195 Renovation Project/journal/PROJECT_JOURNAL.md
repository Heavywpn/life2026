## About This Journal

This is our space to document not just the repairs and tasks, but the feelings, memories, and dreams that make this project meaningful. This boat represents freedom - the freedom to escape, to explore, and to be ourselves on the open water.

## Our Vision: Life on the Water

### What This Boat Means to Us

This isn't just about fixing a boat. It's about reclaiming something precious - our time together, our adventures, and the pure joy of being out on the water.

**Our Dreams:**
- Weekend escapes from the everyday grind
- Sunset cruises with nowhere to be
- Fishing trips and lazy afternoons
- The feeling of wind and spray
- Freedom to just BE

### The Joy of Boating

There's something magical about being on the water. The stress of daily life disappears the moment we pull away from the dock. Out here, it's just us, the water, and endless possibility.

**What We Love Most:**
- The sound of water against the hull
- Kristyn's joy going topless, feeling the sun and freedom
- Finding quiet coves and hidden spots
- The satisfaction of handling the boat together
- Watching the sunset from the water
- The simplicity of boat life - just sun, water, and us
- Creating memories that last forever
