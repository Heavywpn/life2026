# Bayliner 195 Renovation Project

## Project Overview
**Project Managers:** Rick & Kristyn
**Start Date:** November 2025
**Boat:** Bayliner 195
**Location:** Queensland, Australia

### Project Goals
1. **Primary Goal:** Restore the Bayliner 195 to full operational condition for weekly use
2. **Secondary Goal:** Prepare boat for potential sale with enhanced value
3. **Compliance Goal:** Ensure all work meets Australian and Queensland maritime regulations
4. **Personal Goal:** Create lasting memories and reclaim the freedom of the water

---

## Project Vision

This project represents more than just boat repairs - it's about reclaiming our weekends, our freedom, and our connection to the water. Whether we're heading out for a sunset cruise, fishing, or just escaping the everyday routine, this boat symbolizes adventure and possibility.
