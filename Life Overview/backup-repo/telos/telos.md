## Problems

*The three fundamental problems I care about solving:*

Veterans abandoned by the military. People with disabilities can't find work. People trapped in crises with no escape routes. Charities struggling without proper support.

### P2: Integrity and Service Replaced by Greed in Business

IT companies charge "what others charge" instead of value delivered. Service-first replaced by self-interest. Work ethic replaced by entitlement. Lying as default response.

**Why it matters:** I worked for greedy companies. I compete against MSPs overcharging for minimal service. My grandparents taught me service above self. East Timor showed me what happens when greed and power dominate. I cannot build a business model based on exploitation.

### P3: People and Businesses Vulnerable to Cyber Threats

Constant targeting and hacking. They can't defend themselves. Bad actors exploit this. Small businesses and charities especially vulnerable.

**Why it matters:** I have the skills to protect them (firewalls, security, Microsoft expertise). East Timor taught me what exploitation looks like. I cannot stand by while people get victimized when I can protect them.
