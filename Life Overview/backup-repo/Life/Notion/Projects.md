## Weekly Review

---

- â—Ž ==**â‡ **==
    - **Setting The Weekly Review**
        
        For each project make sure you select a Last Review Date and Review Frequency (in days). This will then mean the project will be automatically returned to your on the main dashboard under the on the homepage**.**
        
        E.g. Set it to the current Friday with a review frequency of '7' and you'll see it appear for review the next Friday. You can see how this works fully here: [https://youtu.be/PDRTLxdYaaw](https://youtu.be/PDRTLxdYaaw)
        
    - **Project Dashboards & Views**
        
        - You can find all your Projects on the [[Projects]] Dashboard
        - There is also a view of this Dash on the [[P.A.R.A Dashboard]]
        - Finally, there are filtered views of Live and Highlighted Projects on the main [[Notion Life OS PUBLIC BETA 1.2 Backup]] homepage and the [[Do Today Action View]]
        - There is a page (also on the [[Notion Life OS PUBLIC BETA 1.2 Backup]] Section of the homespage) where you can see all projects marked for Review.
        - For Projects set up as Master Projects or Channels for the You can also see Live Projects on on the Creative OS Dash [HERE](https://www.notion.so/facfa8eddb2f4cddb4f74382448e696e?pvs=21)
