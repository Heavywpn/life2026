> "Win the morning, win the day." - Tim Ferriss

## Morning Routine Checklist:

- Wake up early (ideally before sunrise)
- Make your bed immediately
- Hydrate with a large glass of water
- Meditate or practice mindfulness for 10-20 minutes
- Perform a gratitude exercise (write down 3 things you're grateful for)
- Engage in light exercise or stretching (e.g., yoga, bodyweight exercises)
- Take a cold shower or practice contrast hydrotherapy
- Consume a nutritious breakfast with protein and healthy fats
- Journal or write morning pages to clear your mind
- Read or listen to educational/inspirational content for 15-30 minutes
- Review your goals and set intentions for the day
- Practice visualization of your ideal day or long-term goals
- Engage in breathwork exercises (e.g., box breathing, Wim Hof method)
- Take essential supplements or vitamins
- Spend time in nature or get natural sunlight exposure
- Listen to uplifting music or a motivational podcast
- Perform a quick tidying session in your living space
- Practice affirmations or positive self-talk
- Plan your day and prioritize your most important tasks
- Engage in a creative activity (e.g., drawing, writing, playing an instrument)
