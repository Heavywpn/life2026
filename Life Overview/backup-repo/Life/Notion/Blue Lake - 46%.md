# What did I think.

## Tasting Notes Summary

Blue Lake Whisky, at 46% ABV, presents itself as a refined whisky with a harmonious balance of aromas and flavors. It delivers a warm, inviting profile with distinct notes of vanilla and caramel on the nose, followed by a smooth but bold character that makes it an excellent pre-meal sipper. This whisky is well-suited for both casual enjoyment and celebratory moments, pairing exceptionally well with savory and mildly sweet dishes.

### Key Notes:

1. **Appearance**: Deep amber with golden highlights, suggesting a rich and full-bodied whisky.
2. **Nose**: A warm bouquet with **vanilla**, **caramel butterscotch**, and subtle hints of toasted oak. There's a faint undertone of dried fruits and baking spices.
3. **Palate**:
    - **First Sip**: A warm, tingling sensation that builds gradually. Light on the mid-palate with a slight sweetness.
    - **Body**: Smooth and rounded, with flavors of honey, vanilla, and a trace of citrus zest.
4. **Finish**: Long and warming, with a gentle spice lingering at the back of the throat.
5. **Texture**: Silky and medium-bodied, with a pleasant mouthfeel that coats the palate.
6. **Balance**: Well-balanced between sweetness, warmth, and a slight dryness from the oak influence.
7. **Complexity**: Not overly complex but offers enough depth to keep the experience engaging.
8. **Occasion**: Ideal as a standalone drink before a meal or for contemplative sipping.
9. **Food Pairing Suggestions**:
    - **Savory**: Grilled lamb chops, roasted nuts, or charcuterie with aged cheeses.
    - **Sweet**: Vanilla panna cotta or a light caramel tart.
10. **Overall Impression**: A versatile whisky that appeals to both novice drinkers and seasoned enthusiasts, with its warm and approachable flavor profile.

### Final Thought:

Blue Lake Whisky is a delightful expression that works best as a pre-dinner drink or alongside light, complementary dishes. Its smooth character and warm finish make it a dependable choice for various occasions.
