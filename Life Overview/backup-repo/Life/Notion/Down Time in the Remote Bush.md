# Pasta Instructions

Based on the transcript provided, I can extract the following instructions for making pasta from scratch while camping:

Objectives:

- Make homemade pasta in a camping setting
- Learn to adapt cooking techniques with limited resources
- Understand the basics of pasta dough preparation

Instructions:

1. Gather ingredients: flour, eggs, and salt
2. Create a well in the flour
3. Crack eggs into the well

- Note: Double yolks can occur and are beneficial for richness

1. Add about two teaspoons of salt
2. Mix ingredients together to form a dough
3. Knead the dough, adding more flour if needed
4. Roll out the dough using a rolling pin or improvised tool

- If no rolling pin is available, a tin can be used as a substitute

1. Cut the rolled dough into desired pasta shapes
2. Boil water and cook the pasta for about 4 minutes
3. Prepare a sauce with ingredients like garlic, cherry tomatoes, and bacon
4. Combine cooked pasta with the sauce and serve

Additional tips:

- Allow ingredients to stick to the pan for better caramelization and flavor
- Salt tomatoes directly when cooking
- The pasta may be thicker than machine-made pasta, so adjust cooking time as needed
