**Summary:**

The 2020 La Villette Cabernet Franc presents itself as a light and delicate wine, with subtle nuances that require patience to fully appreciate. Initially overshadowed by a strong ethanol aroma, this wine benefits significantly from aeration, revealing its true character. It pairs wonderfully with red meats, enhancing the overall dining experience with its understated elegance.

**Tasting Notes:**

- Initial nose reveals a strong ethanol presence, suggesting the need for decanting.
- Upon opening, the wine offers a lighter body than expected, reminiscent of a Merlot.
- As it breathes, delicate flavors emerge, making it an excellent companion to a meal rather than the main attraction.
- The wine's profile improves with a slight drop in ambient temperature.
- A well-rounded second glass highlights the benefits of allowing the wine to breathe.
- The wine displays a gentle complexity, with hints of red fruit and subtle spice.
- Its light and airy character make it suitable for those accustomed to heavier wines like Cabernet Sauvignon or Shiraz.
- The wine's elegance is best appreciated alongside a hearty steak.
- Recommended to let the wine rest for at least 30 minutes before serving.
- Its versatility allows for pairing with a variety of dishes, from grilled meats to soft cheeses.

**Food Recommendations:**

- Pair with a grilled ribeye steak for a classic combination.
- Serve alongside roasted lamb with herbs for a complementary flavor profile.
- Enjoy with a soft cheese platter, including Brie or Camembert, to enhance the wine's lightness.
