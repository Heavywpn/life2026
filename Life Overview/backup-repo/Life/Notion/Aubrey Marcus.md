## Bio

Aubrey Marcus is a prominent figure in the realm of health and wellness. As the founder of Onnit, he has pioneered innovative approaches to enhancing physical and mental performance. Known for integrating ancient wisdom with cutting-edge science, Aubrey has helped countless individuals achieve holistic well-being. He is the author of "Own the Day, Own Your Life," a bestseller that provides actionable insights for living a balanced and fulfilling life. Aubrey also hosts the "Aubrey Marcus Podcast," where he explores transformative ideas with experts and thought leaders. His work continues to inspire and empower people to reach their highest potential.
