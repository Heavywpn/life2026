## NOTES FOR POWERPOINT CREATION

**Font Recommendations**:
- Headers: Bold sans-serif (Impact, Arial Black, or Bebas Neue)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (Arial, Calibri, or Open Sans)
- Keep it readable from back of room

**Color Palette**:
- Primary: Dark blue or charcoal (professional, military feel)
- Accent: Orange or red (action, urgency)
- Background: White or light gray for most slides
- Use black background for Slide 20 (the reveal)

**Transitions**:
- Keep minimal and professional
- Use "cut" transition for Slide 1→2 (the stereotype to reality reveal)
- Smooth fades for most others

**Speaker View**:
- Print speaker notes for practice
- Include timing on each slide
- Mark where to pause for effect

**Practice Notes**:
- Time yourself multiple times
- Practice the transitions between sections
- Know where to pause for effect (especially Slide 20 reveal)
- Practice reading the client quote verbatim - make it sound natural
- Own the swearing reference - don't apologize

**Backup Plans**:
- Have slide numbers visible in speaker view
- Print emergency backup cards with key points
- Test all images load properly before presenting
