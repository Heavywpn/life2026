### Five Essential Knots (by Zach)

1. **Bowline** - "Bunny comes out of tree, around tree, back down hole"
   - Use: Making stirrups if you find a mare on an island

2. **Butterfly Loop** - Isolates damaged section of rope
   - Use: Rope stays strong even with damaged section in loop

3. **Sheet Bend** - Joins two ropes (better than square knot)
   - Use: Different size ropes won't slip like square knot might

4. **Taut-Line Hitch** - Adjustable loop
   - Use: Stays under tension but can slide when released

5. **Trucker's Hitch** - Creates mechanical advantage
   - Use: Tightening loads on truck, creates leverage
   - "Nice trucker's hitch" comment in camping video meant a lot
