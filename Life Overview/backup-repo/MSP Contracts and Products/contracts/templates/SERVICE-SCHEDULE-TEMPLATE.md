## 9. TERM AND TERMINATION

### Agreement Duration
- **Initial Term:** [12/24/36] months from Effective Date
- **Renewal:** Automatically renews for 12-month periods unless terminated

### How to End the Agreement

**At End of Term:**
- Either party may give 90 days written notice before term end
- No penalty applies for termination at end of term

**Early Termination by Client:**
- During Initial Term: 50% of remaining fees payable
- During Renewal Term: 90 days notice or 3 months fees (whichever is less)

**Termination for Cause:**
- Either party may terminate immediately for material breach not cured within 30 days
- Immediate termination for insolvency or inability to pay debts

### Transition Assistance
Upon termination, we will:
- Provide up to 30 days transition assistance (additional fees may apply after 10 days)
- Export/provide your data in standard formats
- Remove our tools from your systems
- Return or securely destroy your confidential information
