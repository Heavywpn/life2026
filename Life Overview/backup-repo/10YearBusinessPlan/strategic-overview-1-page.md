## YEAR 1 PRIORITIES (Nov 2025 - Nov 2026)

**Team & Operations:**
- Hire first L1/L2 operations staff (Feb/Mar 2026) at $60k base
- Eliminate 70-80% of Rick's routine help desk work
- Document 10+ core processes (onboarding, vCIO meetings, escalation, QBRs)

**EOS Implementation (Complete by March 2026):**
- Weekly L10 meetings running (Kristyn, Rick, Dean)
- Quarterly Rocks and scorecard tracking
- Accountability Chart defined

**Pricing Transformation:**
- Legacy customers: $45 → $55/seat (22% increase in Q2/Q3)
- New customers: $130-150/seat from Day 1 (no grandfathering)
- Break/fix: Increase to $250/hour (creates conversion urgency)

**Financial Foundation:**
- Implement Profit First methodology (adapted for Australian tax)
- Begin tracking Venturer vs Core revenue separately
- Achieve 18-20% profit margins

**Revenue Growth:**
- Add $150k-200k new ARR from new managed services customers
- Convert 5-10 break/fix customers to managed services ($75k-125k ARR)
