## Important Booking Notes

### Check-in/Check-out Standard Times:
- **Check-in:** Usually 2:00 PM (varies by park)
- **Check-out:** Usually 10:00 AM (varies by park)
- **Aim to arrive:** By 3:00 PM to set up in daylight
- **Pack up morning of departure:** Start by 8:00 AM

### Questions to Ask When Booking:
1. What size is the powered site? (Confirm Hilux fits)
2. What time is check-in/check-out?
3. What's your cancellation policy?
4. Do you have camp kitchen facilities?
5. Is firewood available for purchase? (check fire bans first)
6. Do you allow campfires/BBQs?
7. Is there a dump point?
8. Do you have hot showers? (included or coin-operated?)
9. What's the policy for late arrivals? (if delayed on road)
10. Do you have secure storage? (for valuables)

### Cancellation Policies:
**Record each campground's policy - typically:**
- More than 7 days notice: Full refund
- 2-7 days notice: 50% refund or credit
- Less than 2 days: No refund (peak season strict!)

### Peak Season Considerations:
- December 23-Jan 10 = PEAK SUMMER SCHOOL HOLIDAYS
- Expect higher prices
- Minimum stay requirements possible (especially NYE)
- Strict cancellation policies
- Book as far ahead as possible
- Friday/Saturday nights especially competitive
