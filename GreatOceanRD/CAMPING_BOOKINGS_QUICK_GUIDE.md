## Important Notes

**Powered vs Unpowered:**
- Book POWERED sites (for 12V fridge)
- Usually $10-15 more per night
- Worth it for convenience

**Peak Season Pricing:**
- Expect higher rates Dec-Jan
- NYE will be premium pricing
- Some may require minimum 2-night stays

**Deposits:**
- Most require deposit or full payment upfront
- Peak season bookings are usually non-refundable
- Get cancellation policy in writing

**Check-in Times:**
- Usually 2pm-6pm
- Call ahead if arriving late
- Some have after-hours key pickup

**Camp Kitchens:**
- Ask if they have camp kitchen
- Saves gas and makes cooking easier
- Good way to meet other travelers
