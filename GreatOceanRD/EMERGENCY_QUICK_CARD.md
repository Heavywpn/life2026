## EVACUATION PLAN

**IF FIRE THREATENS:**

1. Don't wait for official warning
2. Leave EARLY
3. Head to: Coast or major town
4. Keep radio on (ABC Local)
5. Full tank of fuel
6. Stay together
7. Take phones, water, documents

**Never drive through smoke!**

---

## SNAKE/SPIDER BITE

**CALL 000 IMMEDIATELY**

**DO:**
1. Keep person still
2. Apply pressure bandage
3. Immobilize limb
4. Mark bite location
5. Note time

**DON'T:**
- Don't wash bite
- Don't cut bite
- Don't apply tourniquet
- Don't try to catch snake

---

## BLUE BOTTLE STING

1. Remove tentacles (don't rub!)
2. Rinse with seawater
3. Apply ice/hot water (whichever helps)
4. If severe: Call 000

---

## VEHICLE BREAKDOWN

1. Move to safe location
2. Hazard lights on
3. Warning triangle out
4. Call roadside assist
5. Stay with vehicle (unless unsafe)

---

## LOST/INJURED IN BUSH

1. **STOP** - Don't wander
2. Call 000
3. Use Emergency+ for location
4. Stay where you are
5. Signal: 3 of anything (whistles, fires, etc.)
6. Shelter from sun
