# Toyota Hilux Camping Packing List
## Brisbane to Adelaide Great Ocean Road Trip - December 2025

---

## Packing Philosophy for Hilux

**Key Principles:**
- Use soft bags (not hard suitcases) - saves space
- Pack in waterproof bags/containers (dust and rain)
- Organize by frequency of use
- Heavy items low and centered
- Keep essentials accessible
- Maximum space efficiency in Hilux tray/canopy

## PACKING TIPS FOR HILUX

### Organization System

**Storage Zones:**

**Zone 1 - Daily Access (Front/Easy Reach):**
- Day bag with essentials
- Water bottles
- Snacks
- Sunscreen
- Camera
- First aid kit
- Maps & camping app

**Zone 2 - Camp Setup (Middle Tray/Canopy):**
- Tent/swag
- Camp chairs & table
- Cooking gear
- Esky/fridge

**Zone 3 - Occasional Use (Back/Deep Storage):**
- Clothing bags
- Spare bedding
- Tools & recovery gear
- Bulk food supplies

**Zone 4 - Roof (if roof rack):**
- Roof top tent
- Rarely used bulky items
- Secure with tie-downs

### Weight Distribution

- **Heavy items LOW and CENTERED** (fridge, esky, tools)
- **Light items UP TOP** (swags, bedding if no roof tent)
- **Balance side-to-side** (for vehicle handling)

### Weatherproofing

- **Everything in waterproof containers or dry bags**
- Dust on unsealed roads is worse than rain
- Hilux canopy should seal well but double-bag important items
- Silica gel packs in electronics boxes

### Security

- **Lock canopy/tonneau** always when leaving vehicle
- **Valuables inside cab** when possible
- **Hide expensive gear** (cameras, laptops) under other items
- **Don't advertise gear** visible through windows

## Final Notes

### Hilux Load Capacity

- Check your Hilux payload capacity
- Typical Hilux payload: 800-1,000kg
- Don't exceed - affects handling & safety
- Distribute weight properly

### December Heat Considerations

- Extra water storage (3-5L per person per day)
- Shade structures essential
- Electric cool box/fans for sleeping comfort
- Check weather daily (heatwaves possible)
- Have cooling strategies (wet towels, spray bottles)

### Bush Fire Season

- December = Fire season
- Check fire bans DAILY
- No campfires on Total Fire Ban days
- Have evacuation plan
- Download fire alert apps
- Listen to local radio (ABC Local)
