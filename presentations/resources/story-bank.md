## Notes on Storytelling

### Structure
- Start in the middle (action)
- Add context as needed
- Clear problem/resolution
- Connect to audience

### Delivery Tips
- Practice different lengths (elevator to keynote)
- Have visual anchors
- Know where to pause
- Practice the callback

### What Makes a Good Business Story
- Authentic/personal
- Relevant to audience
- Shows values in action
- Has clear takeaway
- Memorable imagery
